Sæhrímnir's Feast

Description

A legendary roast from the boar that heals indefinitely, served endless to the fallen soldiers in Valhalla

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Thyme
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Apple Cider
  • Sea Salt

Steps to Cook

  1. Rub pork on spices and garlic and let it rest
  2. Sear on high heat at 160 degrees for three hours
  3. Baste Every three minutes and finish with honey-thyme
