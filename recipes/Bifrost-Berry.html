Bifrost Berry Elixr

Description

A rainbow layered drinkin sparkling with energy representing the rainbow bridge

Ingredients

  • Strawberry Juice
  • BlackCurrant Juice
  • Bluberry Juice
  • Honey
  • Lemon Juice
  • Mint Leaves
  • Ice Cubes

Steps to Cook

  1. Blend Three berry juices separately with honey and lemon
  2. Chill and layer in a tall glass using spoon
  3. Top with mint leaves
