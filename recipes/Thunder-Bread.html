Thunderbread with lightning butter

Description

Baked in thor's honor, soft inside paired with spicy butter kicks like mjolnir

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Flour
  • Rye
  • Yeast
  • Ale
  • Sour Cream
  • Molasses
  • Caraway Seeds

Steps to Cook

  1. Mix wheat rye ale and sourcream, then sprinkle some caraway seeds
  2. Bake at 200 Degree Celsius for 40 minutes
  3. Whip butter, crushed red pepper, mustard seeds and lemon zest
